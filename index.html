Ask why a position is strong, explore alternative strategies, or get personalized advice based on your drill history. Coaching that adapts to how you actually play.

Why is splitting better than slotting here?
Great question. With this roll, splitting to the 18-point creates two anchoring chances in your opponent's home board. Slotting the 7-point is aggressive but leaves a direct shot — and you're behind in the race by 14 pips.
The equity swings -0.229 because you're giving up the flexibility of having a back checker in play. Think of it this way: you can always make the 7-point later, but you can't un-hit a blot.
